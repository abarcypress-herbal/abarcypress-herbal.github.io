Welcome to AberCypress

 

Official Representative of Iran’s Leading Brands in Europe

At AberCypress, we are a specialized merchant company proudly serving as the official and exclusive representative of Iran’s most trusted brands in the field of high-quality herbal and food products. Our mission is to bridge Iranian producers and European markets, fostering strong, reliable, and long-term business relationships — with a focus on quality, international standards, and professional service.

 

What Sets Us Apart:

 

🌿 Representing the Best of Iran
We partner with top Iranian manufacturers to bring you authentic, premium herbal and food products, carefully sourced to preserve their natural qualities and exceptional value.

 

🤝 A Trusted Link Between Iran and Europe
As the official representative of renowned Iranian brands, AberCypress acts as a reliable commercial gateway, simplifying export, import, and distribution across both regions.

 

🔬 Commitment to Quality and Global Standards
All our products are inspected, packaged, and exported under the guidance of specialists to ensure they fully comply with international quality and safety regulations.

 

🚀 A Long-Term Partnership Approach
We are committed to building mutually beneficial relationships with our European clients — from boutique herbal stores to major food distributors and retailers.
