🌱 Product Overview

At AbarCypress, we offer high-quality Lavender, cultivated in the clean, dry climates of Iran's highland regions. Known for its calming fragrance, essential oil content, and therapeutic properties, lavender is a versatile herb used in numerous industries across Europe.

Our lavender is carefully harvested and gently dried to preserve its natural color, aroma, and active compounds. Whether you're producing herbal teas, cosmetics, essential oils, or aromatherapy products, our lavender delivers purity and potency you can trust.

✅ Key Features

  • High content of linalool and linalyl acetate
  • Clean, chemical-free cultivation
  • Available in whole flowers, cut & sifted, and powder
  • Vivid purple color and strong natural fragrance
  • Fully compliant with EU import and quality standards

🌍 Grown in Iran, Delivered to Europe

We export directly from Iran to clients across Germany and Europe, offering custom packaging, bulk quantities, and professional documentation for a smooth import process. Our team ensures every shipment meets the expectations of herbal, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical industries.

💼 Applications

  • Herbal and wellness teas
  • Aromatherapy and essential oil extraction
  • Sleep and stress-relief formulations
  • Natural skincare and beauty products
  • Home fragrance and bath products

📦 Packaging Options

  • 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, and 25kg bulk bags
  • Vacuum-sealed or eco-friendly kraft packaging
  • Custom labeling and private brand options available
